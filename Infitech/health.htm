This may seem as an anomaly but it is possible to provide this remedy for community considered inferior in the society. They are equipped to aid the people with every possible action that they are urged to do.Most imporatantly these cyborgs are highly advanced and do not influence the society to think of the disabled as inferior and neglectable. Cyborgs are highly equipped to perform the normal activities of a normal person. Therefore it can be used by all without worry. Also the technology of the cyborgs are over the top. However, cyborgs are highly expensive due to the advanced technology incorporated in the contraption and the high quality raw materials used to devleop the project.
Augmented Reality
The brigde between reality and fascination.

Augmented reality is the way of which artificial graphics and animations can be overlayed over a real graphic taken seperately from our surrounding. Augmented Reality-AR for short-can be used in diagnosis of diseases by overlaying the condition of on-going diagnoses. For example, a model of a bone or a muscle can be overlayed on the natural, real picture. Or, a model of a pulmonary artery which carries oxygen from the right ventricle of the heart to the lungs can be overlayed. AR accelerates convenience in the disease diagnosis process because it enables the construction of three-dimensional graphics which are overlayed on the natural picture so the artificial internal graphic of the organ in diagnosis can be done. However, AR also needs experience in interaction to achieve its full potential in work.
