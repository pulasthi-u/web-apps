Scandium

  • Scandium was one of the elements predicted by Mendeleev and later confirmed by its discovery
  • A lightweight metal, that is fairly resistant to corrosion
  • Has a higher melting point than \(\ce{Al}\), and is therefore used in the aerospace industry over \(\ce{Al}\), where resistance to high temperatures is required
  • Scandium is also used to make bicycle frames and sports equipment
  • The only ion formed by Scandium is \(\ce{Sc^3+}\), which has no \(\ce{d}\) electrons, and is therefore colorless.
